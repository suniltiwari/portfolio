About

I'm an independent ASP.NET/C# developer working remotely with businesses on a freelance basis. 15+ years split between long-term enterprise roles and direct client work — building HR systems, booking engines, internal tools, and reporting dashboards.

I work best with businesses that already have a system in place — one that's slow, hard to maintain, or just needs a dedicated pair of hands — rather than a blank slate. My core stack is ASP.NET, C#, and SQL Server, backed by REST APIs and deployed on IIS. I've also built background verification (BGV) workflow systems, so I understand this space from both the software and the process side.

Engagements are hourly, fixed-price per project, or a monthly retainer for ongoing support — whichever fits how you need to work.
